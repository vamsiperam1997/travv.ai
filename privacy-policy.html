Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

Travv™ AI Inc. ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Travv mobile application and website at travv.ai (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the Service.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Location Data

  • Real-time GPS location to provide navigation and route planning
  • GPS coordinates to display attractions and points of interest along your route
  • Location history for saved trips and planning features
  • Background location (only when you grant permission) for live navigation

1.2 Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and account credentials
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Billing and payment information (processed securely via Apple/Google)
  • Feedback, reviews, and survey responses

1.3 Travel Booking Data

  • Flight search queries and booking details
  • Hotel and motel search queries and reservations
  • Car rental search queries and reservations
  • Traveler names, dates of travel, and preferences

1.4 Usage Data

  • App interaction data and session duration
  • Trip history and saved routes
  • Search queries within the app
  • Premium feature usage and subscription status

1.5 Device Information

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version and crash/error reports
  • IP address and network connection type

1.6 Voice Data

  • Voice commands when you use the voice control feature (processed locally and/or via third-party speech services)
  • We do not store raw audio recordings beyond what is required for processing

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide navigation, route planning, and location-based services
  • Display tourist attractions, hotels, flights, and car rentals along your routes
  • Process travel bookings (flights, hotels, car rentals) on your behalf
  • Process and manage premium subscription payments
  • Respond to voice commands and provide AI-powered recommendations
  • Improve app functionality, performance, and user experience
  • Send service-related notifications, booking confirmations, and updates
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

3. SHARING YOUR INFORMATION

We may share your information with:

3.1 Travel Partners

  • Airlines, hotels, and car rental companies to complete bookings you request
  • Travel booking API partners (e.g., Amadeus, Booking.com, Duffel) to search and process reservations
  • Only the minimum data necessary to complete your booking is shared

3.2 Service Providers

  • Google Maps Platform — mapping and location services
  • RevenueCat — subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Google Gemini AI — AI-powered content and recommendations
  • Supabase — data storage and authentication
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — payment processing

3.3 Legal Requirements

  • When required by law, court order, or government authority
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Travv™ AI Inc., our users, or the public

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for advertising purposes.

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

  • Data is stored on secure, encrypted servers
  • We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for data in transit
  • Payment information is never stored on our servers — processed by Apple/Google
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
  • You can request deletion of your account and data at any time

5. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out — Opt out of certain data collection (may limit app functionality)
  • Location — Revoke location permissions at any time via device settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@travv.ai

6. CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at support@travv.ai.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Service is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at support@travv.ai.

8. INTERNATIONAL USERS

Travv™ AI Inc. is based in the United States. If you access our Service from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from your country. By using our Service, you consent to this transfer.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, through in-app notification. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
