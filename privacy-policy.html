Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Travv operates the Travv mobile application (the "Travv"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Location Data

  • Real-time location data to provide navigation and route planning
  • GPS coordinates to display attractions along your route
  • Location history for trip planning features

1.2 Personal Information

  • Email address and account credentials
  • Profile information you provide
  • Feedback and survey responses

1.3 Usage Data

  • App interaction data
  • Trip history and saved routes
  • Premium feature usage

1.4 Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version and crash reports

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use collected information to:

  • Provide navigation and route planning services
  • Display tourist attractions along your routes
  • Process premium subscriptions
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Respond to user feedback

  • Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

  • Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

3.4 Supabase

  • Hosts user data and authentication
  • Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

  • Data is stored on secure servers
  • We use encryption for sensitive information
  • Location data is processed in real-time and stored securely
  • You can delete your account and associated data at any time

5. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data

  • Delete your account and data

Our App is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
